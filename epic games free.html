Epic Games Free Games

Epic Games is a video game development company based in Cary, North Carolina. The company was founded in 1991 by Tim Sweeney, and it initially focused on developing shareware games for the PC platform. Since then, Epic Games has evolved into a major player in the gaming industry, with a focus on developing and publishing high-quality, visually stunning games for a wide range of platforms.

Epic Games is known for its generosity in offering free games to its users. Since 2019, the company has been offering free games every week through its online store, Epic Games Store. This initiative is called the "Epic Games Store Free Games Program," and it has been hugely popular among gamers.

Dying Light Enhanced Edition

Dying Light Enhanced Edition is an action-packed, first-person survival horror game developed by Techland and published by Warner Bros. Interactive Entertainment. The game is an expanded version of the original Dying Light game, released in 2015, and includes all the DLCs and additional content released for the game.

The game is set in a post-apocalyptic world overrun by zombies, where the player takes on the role of Kyle Crane, an undercover agent who is sent to infiltrate a quarantine zone in the city of Harran. The game is played from a first-person perspective, and the player is free to explore the open world environment of the city, which is filled with both friendly and hostile NPCs, as well as hordes of zombies.

Shapez.io

Shapez is a puzzle game developed and published by Tobias Springer. The game challenges players to create complex shapes using a variety of tools and techniques. It is available on Steam and has gained popularity among puzzle game enthusiasts.

The gameplay in Shapez is based on a simple concept: players are given a blank canvas and a set of basic shapes, such as squares, triangles, and circles. Using these shapes, players must create more complex shapes by combining, resizing, and rotating them. As players progress through the game, they unlock new tools and abilities, such as the ability to split shapes, merge them, or change their color.
